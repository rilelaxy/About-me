My Goals and Inspirations:

I am very dedicated.

Big Dreams!

As you can tell from my favorites section, I really love Nintendo.
When I was 3 years old, I recieved my first console, a DS Lite.
I played it everyday even though I couldn't read, it always made me feel passionate and happy.
That is when I was inspired to provide the same joy to other kids.
This sounds absurd, but I told my mom at 3 that I wanted to be CEO of Nintendo of America.
As I got older, I began working hard to reach my goal, and found that I love game design, especially UX and narrative design.
I have never once doubted it is what I want to do, and what I will do.
I want to help others, the same way others have helped me.
Wether that's through video games, creating policies with the city, or even just spending time with my friends.
It's what I consider my life purpose, and it brings me happiness everyday. I love what I do.
I have been confident in that since I could think.
